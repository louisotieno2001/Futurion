+ Know more about us and our programs and missions Here

As a new service, FuturionLab is committed to making this world a better place for the present and future generations. Our committements range from the world of technology in making an inclusive tech space to bringing to life new technologies that will make life a better experience.

As part of our inclusive-tech initiative, we are working on a build script that will enable android apps to be built almost on any text editor. The Build On Basics script (BOB Script). If you would love to contribute, get in touch and we'll send you an invite to our currently private repo Contribute

Our other committements are in the space of climate and climate change, we try as much as possible to keep in mind the increasing concerns into the state the climate crisis has reached and are committed to developing solutions to the problems we face today to make for a better tomorrow.

As part of addressing climate change, we are delving into tech that greatly reduces energy consumption while still giving out the best perfomance. On this, we are currently working on our screenless television. Know more about our other incredibly inmpossible ideas by getting in touch Get more info
